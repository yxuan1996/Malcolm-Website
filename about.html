About the author

Malcolm Mejin is not your average writer—he's a master of mayhem, a connoisseur of chaos, and a champion of chuckles! With a wild imagination and a pen that dances between the lines of reality and fantasy, Malcolm weaves tales that are equal parts hilarious and heartwarming.

When he's not busy creating worlds where magic runs amok, you might find him plotting his next epic story over a cup of coffee. Buckle up and get ready for a rollercoaster ride through the whimsical worlds of Malcolm Mejin—where anything can happen, and usually does!
